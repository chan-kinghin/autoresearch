# Research Program: LLM-Based Autonomous Agents

## Topic
Survey recent advances in LLM-based autonomous agents (2023-2026).

## Research Questions
1. What are the main architectural patterns for LLM agents (ReAct, plan-and-execute, tree-of-thought, etc.)?
2. How do modern agent frameworks (LangGraph, CrewAI, AutoGen, Claude Agent SDK) compare in design philosophy?
3. What are the key challenges in agent reliability, safety, and evaluation?
4. What real-world applications have demonstrated practical value?
5. What are the emerging trends for 2025-2026?

## Scope
- Focus on autonomous agents (not simple chatbots or RAG pipelines)
- Include both academic papers and industry implementations
- Cover open-source frameworks and commercial products
- Time range: 2023-2026

## Constraints
- Prioritize peer-reviewed or well-cited sources
- Include specific framework comparisons where possible
- Note limitations and failure modes, not just capabilities
